Step 1   ➟   Step 2   ➟   Step 3

  • First Step

    Here you would select your colour of your frame, size of your frame, and if it's frameset or complete.

    If it's frameset only, the next step button would be just add to cart and would skip the rest of the process
    If they select complete bike, only once all the options (colour, size, complete/frameset) are selected will the "next step" button be available
    They also won't be able to go to next steps through the links above until they select all required options in the previous step

  • Second Step

    Here they would select groupset, crankset, and cassette options.
    Again, if not all options are selected, they cannot go to the next step and the button will be disabled until they do

  • Third Step

    Here they will select handlebar and stem options
    If this were the last step (which it's not, it's just an example) they could add it to the cart now
